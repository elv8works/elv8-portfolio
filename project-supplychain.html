Supply Chain Management

A smart solution to track and manage gold inventory with automation.

Problem Statement

Businesses in the gold sector faced difficulty in tracking real-time inventory, payments, and returns. Manual processes often caused delays and errors.

Steps Taken

  • Analyzed existing workflows and identified inefficiencies.
  • Designed a centralized Google Sheets system.
  • Implemented automation using Apps Script.
  • Built real-time reporting dashboards.

Tools Used

Google Sheets Apps Script Automation

Outcome

A live automated system reduced manual effort by 70%, improved accuracy, and enabled businesses to make faster, data-driven decisions.
